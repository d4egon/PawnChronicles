Possible things to add:

Two-pawn arcs could evolve into the PsychicBond - a gene hediff that exists in biotech ?
Addiction arcs: dedicated arc track per addiction type (alcohol, smokeleaf,
wake-up, go-juice, luciferium, flake, yayo, etc.). Arc follows the pawn through
dependency, withdrawal, and either relapse or recovery. Successful completion
triggers a cure removing the addiction hediff and grants a matching narrative
epithet. Failure leaves the addiction and gives a corresponding epithet.

Crafted condition categories (deferred Bug 6): split the combined
ThingsConstructed + ThingsCrafted counter into per-workbench categories (art
bench, tailoring, construction, drug lab). Requires one Harmony patch and one
counter field per work type.

Luciferium arc (separate track - more complex than other addiction arcs):
  - Unique 8-stage structure: opening, dependency, reckoning, quest, danger,
    crisis, success, failure.
  - Hard road fires a custom incident generating an AncientDanger-type world
    site with a HealerMechSerum guaranteed in loot, anchored to the pawn.
  - New wait conditions: wait_condition_site_generated (site spawned),
    wait_condition_site_cleared (pawn cleared it).
  - Harmony patch: detect HealerMechSerum used on the pawn OR site cleared.
  - Failure path fires automatically if luciferium supply runs out before
    site is cleared (withdrawal imminent check).
  - Tone: expedition log, not recovery story. What the pawn found in the dark,
    what they had to do to get the serum, what they left behind.
  - RemoveHediff on success targets Addiction_Luciferium specifically.

Modded drug support improvements:
  - Inject [pc_addiction_name] as a grammar rule at resolve time in TriggerNextStage
    for fixed addiction arcs. Get the name from ChemicalDef.label (linked via
    ChemicalDef.addictionHediff == hediffDef), fallback to stripping " addiction"
    from HediffDef.label. Update chemical fallback grammar entries to use
    [pc_addiction_name] instead of hardcoded "the compound".
  - Add retroactive check in Patch_Game_FinalizeInit: after normal EvaluateAndStartEpic,
    walk each colonist's hediff set for any Hediff_Addiction and call
    TryStartAddictionArc if no arc is running. Covers pawns already addicted on save load.
  - Edge case: mods that implement addiction via a custom class NOT extending
    Hediff_Addiction will bypass Patch 15's `is Hediff_Addiction` check. If this
    becomes common (e.g. Odyssey ships custom mushroom hediff classes), widen the
    check to also match HediffDef.hediffClass assignability or a custom tag/flag.
  - Test against Odyssey mushroom content when released.
